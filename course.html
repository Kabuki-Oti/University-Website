Courses We Offer

Search for undergraduate courses, masters and research degrees

Undergraduate
courses

Bachelors (or honours, styled as ‘Hons’) degrees are Level 6 courses. They include titles like Bachelor of Arts, or BA (Hons); Bachelor of Science, or BSc (Hons); Bachelor of Engineering, or BEng (Hons); and Bachelor of Law, or LLB.

*Sometimes, these degrees include an initial year of foundation-level study to help you develop the tools you need to progress into your degree, which are normally titled as degrees ‘with foundation year’.

Masters
courses

Looking to progress from your current role, change industries with an accredited qualification, or simply explore the subject you love in the company of research experts?

There are many motivations for studying beyond your degree — find out what the University of Leeds can offer you.

Research
degrees

The two types of research degrees are:

Research master's degrees: MPhil (Master of Philosophy) and MRes (Master of Research)

Doctoral degrees: PhD (Doctor in Philosophy), MD (Doctor of Medicine), and various Professional Doctorates

Our facilities

Our beautiful campus offers the perfect environment to learn and live in. With everything you need close at hand, and fantastic facilities and services to support you.
You'll be right at home!

World's class library

With two University libraries on campus, the Sydney Jones library and the Harold Cohen library, you will have 24/7 access to books, computers, study spaces and library support.

Largest playground

Whatever your sporting interests, we have a dedicated and growing team of professional staff to help you get the best out of our excellent facilities.

Tasty and healthy food

Great Food at Leeds is a University service and any profits that are made are invested back into further improving the cafeteria service and contribute towards the wider University of Leeds.
